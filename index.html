Bart Smeets

I am a researcher working at the interface of active matter physics and mechanobiology. I love building computational models of multicellular systems. For this, we mainly develop agent-based models focusing on mechanics that represent interaction between cells and subcellular elements. In our group, we have been spearheading the development of detailed foam-like descriptions of tissues. These models are applied to various biological and technological processes, including embryonal development, tumor growth, in biofabrication operations (such as 3D printing), or in bacterial biofilms.
